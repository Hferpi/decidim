# Decidim

Decidim is a SaaS (Software as a Service) application designed to modernize and digitalize voting processes in educational institutions. It transforms traditional systems into an agile, secure, and accessible experience.

The platform offers a simple and intuitive interface, anonymous voting, real-time counting, management of multiple simultaneous votes, and notifications to keep users informed about events and results.

The platform is specifically aimed at educational centers wishing to automate and optimize their voting processes, such as:
